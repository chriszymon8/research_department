About The Gentry

Gentry Timepieces is a distinguished establishment in the world of luxury watches, catering to discerning clients who appreciate the finer things in life. With a focus on pre-owned timepieces from renowned brands such as Rolex, Omega, Cartier, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Tudor, IWC, and many others, we stand as a trusted destination for those seeking quality, style, and authenticity in their horological acquisitions.

Our Mission

At Gentry Timepieces, we not only sell watches but also cultivate a community of watch enthusiasts who share our love for craftsmanship and heritage. Our dedication to authenticity, integrity, and customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do, as we believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device – it is a reflection of one's style, personality, and journey through life.watch experts is here to assist you every step of the way.
